What's this then?

At the start of the year I thought it'd be nice to have a place for all the little things I do on a day to day basis. The stuff that doesn't really deserve long a winded blog post, but nice enough to warrant having a place on this great world wide museum that is the web.

...it's just a Facebook Album

Kinda. I didn't realize how similiar it was until things started working. Oh well. I can customize this a heckuva lot more and the project has served as good practice for web development. I'm content that people might 'like' something without hitting a button to show it.

Anno?

Anno is Latin for "In the Year Of", which is exactly what this is: Dylan in the year of 2016.

In truth I wanted to name this something better than just "Calendar". I hope it doesn't come off as pretentious! Anno was a nice, concise, interesting, and spot on term for it.

As always, Enjoy!
